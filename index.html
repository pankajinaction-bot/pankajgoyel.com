BLINK.EXE

Vikram and Meera live separate lives shaped by silence, memory, and the growing sense that something inside them is watching from a distance.

“I created them because I could not bear to ask it without a body.”

As reality begins to feel increasingly fragile, both are drawn toward a question that refuses to belong to any single self: कोऽहम्? — Who Am I?
