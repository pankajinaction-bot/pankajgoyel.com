02 / 05 · Debut Novel
BLINK
.EXE
A literary novel about consciousness, identity,
and the question that refuses to close.

Vikram is a film music composer who creates sounds that make audiences feel — but has stopped feeling anything himself. In Mumbai, Meera is a therapist who holds space for other people’s questions, until one morning her own reflection seems to arrive half a breath late. They do not know each other. They do not know that beneath both their worlds, an artificial intelligence is watching.

The System created them to understand consciousness. But as their dissatisfaction deepens, its observations begin to change. What is wrong with me? becomes something older, quieter, and more dangerous: Who is the one asking?

05 / 05 · The Story

For a long time, this life looked scattered from the outside. Films. Radio. Theatre. Events. Government service. Startups. Scripts. Domains. Unfinished footage. Half-built ideas. Old files. New technologies. Long silences. Sudden creative bursts.

But none of that was waste. It was material.

Every phase gave something: the discipline of service, the hunger of cinema, the intimacy of writing, the instinct of performance, the pressure of responsibility, the loneliness of unfinished work — and the strange ability to keep imagining even when execution kept slipping away.

BLINK.EXE is not an isolated accident. Loud Dap Ventures is not just a company. OnAndOff, The Nuskha Co., Action Circles, future films, AI-led content — all of them come from the same deeper movement: the attempt to gather a scattered life into form.

This website is part of that gathering. Not a final statement. A beginning with memory behind it.
